Express Model Type

One step assessment process

You only prove yourself once to get access to real funds. You just need to hit the growth target at your own pace and get access to real funds. As simple as that!

15% Profit share from the FundedNext Challenge phase

Whatever amount you profit each month during the assessment phase, 15% of it is yours. So you start earning a profit share as a reward even when you’re trading the FundedNext Challenge account.

No Unrealistic profit targets

Lesser the restrictions, better the performance. Keeping that in mind, we decided to ask for only a 25% growth target. You trade at your own pace without any pressure of reaching any absurd targets.

No time limit

Have absolute freedom while you trade. If you sign up for our express model, take as much time as you need to reach your target without feeling the pressure of running out of time.

Upto 90% profit sharing

After getting a funded account, you will start with a 60% profit split to begin with, and then, with each successful withdrawal, your profit share increases all the way up to 90%.

Funding in as fast as 10 trading days

Hit the account growth target during the assessment phase and get access to the real funded account in as low as 10 trading days from signup.
